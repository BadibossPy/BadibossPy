GeoAI & Water Systems

I work at the intersection of hydrological modeling and geospatial AI. My focus includes flood risk assessment using ERA5 climate reanalysis, processing multi-temporal Sentinel-1/2 imagery for water body detection, and building scalable pipelines for NetCDF and GRIB data. Projects range from real-time flood monitoring to long-term climate impact analysis on water resources.

Flood Risk Modeling Platform

Integrated DEM analysis, precipitation forecasts, and hydrological simulation to generate real-time flood hazard maps. Processes 10+ years of ERA5 data with automated COG generation for web delivery.

xarray GDAL PostGIS NetCDF COG

Sentinel-Based Water Mapping

Automated water body extraction from Sentinel-1 SAR and Sentinel-2 optical imagery. Implements NDWI, MNDWI, and ML-based classification with temporal analysis for seasonal variability.

Sentinel Hub Rasterio scikit-learn Dask

Climate Data Processing Engine

Scalable pipeline for ingesting, transforming, and serving ERA5 and CMIP6 climate data. Handles multi-dimensional arrays, performs spatial aggregation, and exports to multiple formats (Zarr, COG, GeoTIFF).

xarray Zarr SLURM AWS S3

Geospatial Engineering & GeoSaaS

Building production geospatial systems requires more than analysis—it demands robust data infrastructure. I design and implement PostGIS databases, STAC-compliant catalogs, FastAPI-based services, and cloud-optimized data pipelines. These systems power decision-making tools for energy, water, and urban planning.

Flood Risk Data Lake

Multi-source spatial data warehouse integrating DEM, land cover, precipitation, and river network data. Built on PostGIS with automated ingestion, versioning, and REST API for querying flood hazard zones.

PostgreSQL PostGIS FastAPI Docker

Hydropower GeoSaaS Toolkit

End-to-end platform for hydropower resource assessment. Processes hydrological time series, performs turbine selection optimization, and generates production forecasts. Includes web dashboard and QGIS plugin for site planning.

PyQGIS FastAPI TimescaleDB Plotly

Settlement Electrification Optimization – Benin 2040

Geospatial optimization model for least-cost electrification planning. Combines population density, grid proximity, solar irradiance, and terrain analysis to determine optimal grid extension vs. off-grid solutions.
